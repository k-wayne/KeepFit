Arms Workout

How to do it:Grab a pair of dumbbells and let them hang at arm's length next to your sides with your palms facing your thighs.Without moving your upper arms, bend your elbow and curl the dumbbells as close to your shoulders as you can.Pause,then slowly lower the weight back to the starting position.Each time you return to the starting position,completely straighten your arms.

Abs Workout

How to do it:Sit on a mat with you hands at the back and your waist tilted backwards at a 45 degrees angle.Then criss-cross your legs one after the other.This would ultimately lead to formation of abs!

Lower Body Workout

How to do it:With your arms behind your head, stretch one your legs behind and then kneel down with the other.Repeat the excercise several times.

Upper Body Workout

How to do it:On a mat, lie on your back and make sure you are on your toes.Using your hands raise your upper body and drop back down to the mat.
